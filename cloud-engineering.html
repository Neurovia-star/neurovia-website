Cloud Engineering Program

Duration: 2 Months

Price: ₦170,000

Build practical cloud skills for AWS and Azure, including deployment, networking, storage, and automation for real-world environments.

Curriculum

  • Week 1 - Cloud Fundamentals & Career Paths

    Understand how modern cloud platforms power business operations and which roles are in demand across startup and enterprise teams.

  • Week 2 - Compute, Networking & Virtual Machines

    Learn how servers, networks, and traffic routing work in the cloud so you can build reliable systems from the ground up.

  • Week 3 - Storage, Databases & Data Security

    Explore how cloud data is stored, backed up, and protected so your applications stay available and compliant.

  • Week 4 - IAM, Access Control & Governance

    Master identity management and access policies that prevent unauthorized use and support secure team collaboration.

  • Week 5 - Deployment Pipelines & DevOps Basics

    Build practical skills in automating deployment, testing, and release workflows that save time and reduce human error.

  • Week 6 - Infrastructure as Code

    Use automation tools to provision and manage infrastructure efficiently, a must-have skill for modern cloud teams.

  • Week 7 - Monitoring, Scaling & Cost Optimization

    Learn how successful teams keep systems healthy, responsive, and cost-effective while growing their platforms.

  • Week 8 - Capstone Cloud Project

    Deploy a complete cloud solution that demonstrates your ability to build, secure, and manage a real-world environment.
