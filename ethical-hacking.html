Ethical Hacking & Bug Bounty

Duration: 2.5 Months

Price: ₦180,000

Learn how to discover vulnerabilities, perform web application testing, and report issues responsibly using industry-standard tools.

Curriculum

  • Week 1 - Linux & Web Security Foundations

    Get comfortable with the tools and environments used by security professionals, from Linux commands to web architecture basics.

  • Week 2 - Reconnaissance & Target Mapping

    Learn how attackers gather information, map targets, and identify exposure points before launching any test.

  • Week 3 - Web App Security Essentials

    Understand the common weaknesses in websites and apps that lead to data leaks, account takeover, and business risk.

  • Week 4 - Injection, XSS & Broken Access Controls

    Practice testing for some of the most damaging vulnerabilities seen in real applications and bug bounty programs.

  • Week 5 - Authentication, APIs & Session Security

    Explore how sessions, tokens, and APIs are abused and how strong design prevents unauthorized access.

  • Week 6 - Bug Bounty Workflow & Reporting

    Learn how to document findings professionally, prioritize risk, and communicate vulnerabilities in a way companies actually understand.

  • Week 7 - Privilege Escalation & Real-World Testing

    Discover how attackers move from low-level access to high-value systems and how defenders can close those gaps.

  • Week 8 - Capstone Security Assessment

    Complete a full security review of a sample platform and deliver a report that looks like something a real consulting team would submit.
