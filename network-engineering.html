Network Engineering Program

Duration: 2 Months

Price: ₦150,000

Build the skills to design, configure, secure, and troubleshoot modern networks for homes, campuses, and enterprise systems.

Curriculum

  • Week 1 - Networking Fundamentals

    Understand IP addressing, subnetting, switching, and the role of routers in modern communication systems.

  • Week 2 - Routing & Switching

    Learn how data travels across networks and how routers and switches keep traffic flowing efficiently.

  • Week 3 - Wireless & LAN Design

    Set up robust local and wireless networks with strong coverage, reliability, and performance.

  • Week 4 - Network Security Basics

    Protect networks with firewalls, secure access rules, and basic threat defense strategies.

  • Week 5 - Troubleshooting & Monitoring

    Diagnose common connectivity issues and use monitoring tools to keep systems healthy.

  • Week 6 - Capstone Network Project

    Design and document a complete network solution for a realistic business or campus environment.
